George Brown College

Location: 
    George Brown College is located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.

Overview: 
    George Brown College is a well-respected institution of higher education in Canada.
    It is known for its commitment to providing high-quality, career-focused education and training to its students. 
    The college offers a wide range of programs, including diploma, degree, and certificate programs, across various fields of study.

Campuses: 
    George Brown College has multiple campuses in Toronto, each specializing in different areas of study. 
    Some of its prominent campuses include the St. James Campus, Waterfront Campus, and Casa Loma Campus.
