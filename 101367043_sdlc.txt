Why learning being a DevOps engineer is important?

    In the industry where continuous learning is integral, learning to being a DevOps engineer encompasses technical skills, soft skills,
    and the ability to adapt to a rapidly changing IT landscape. DevOps engineers who prioritize learning are better equipped to drive innovation, 
    enhance collaboration, and optimize software delivery pipelines in their organizations. 


In addition, part of the DevOps engineer's work is Software Development Life Cycle:

    DevOps primarily focuses on the automation, collaboration, and optimization of software delivery processes, it is closely intertwined with the SDLC. 
    DevOps engineers play a critical role in aligning the practices and tools used in both development and operations to ensure that software is delivered 
    efficiently, reliably, and securely throughout its lifecycle.
