Why is learning Software Development Life Cycle Important

    Learning the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is crucial for effective software development. 
    It provides a structured approach to planning, designing, developing, testing, and deploying software projects, 
    leading to improved efficiency, quality, and cost management. SDLC methodologies ensure clear communication, risk mitigation, 
    and client satisfaction while allowing for flexibility to adapt to project requirements. This knowledge is essential for software 
    professionals, as it enhances career prospects and fosters a culture of continuous improvement in software development processes.
