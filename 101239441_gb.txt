George Brown College has multiple campuses all around Toronto. George Brown offers more than 170 full-time
programs in art and design, business, community services, early childhood education, construction and engineering
technologies, health sciences, hospitality and culinary arts, preparatory studies, as well as specialized programs
and services for recent immigrants and international students.
George Brown has a diverse group of individuals that are on campus. You will be able to find people you relate to
no matter your ethnicity. It is a place where you create connections that will last a lifetime.
The atmosphere inside each campus is welcoming, and each campus is near the Downtown Core allowing you to
explore the city.
